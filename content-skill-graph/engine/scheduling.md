## Batch Workflow

Weekly Batch (Sunday or Monday):

1. Choose 2-3 topics for the week (upcoming shows, recent recaps, artist news).
2. Run the full [Repurpose](repurpose.md) chain for each topic.
3. This gives you 6-9 posts for the whole week in one sitting.
4. Schedule everything using your scheduler (Buffer, Later, or native schedulers).
5. Create Facebook Events for any upcoming shows.

Daily (10 min):

1. Check engagement across all three platforms.
2. Reply to comments — especially Facebook discussions and Instagram DMs
   asking about tickets.
3. Note what hook types or topics drove the most reach.

Weekly Review (Friday):

1. Which shows or acts drove the most engagement? → More of those.
2. What hook types performed best per platform? → Update [Hooks](hooks.md).
3. Any buyer inquiries or DMs that came through social? → Follow up.
4. Any upcoming shows that need more promotion push next week?

## Show-Specific Cadence

For each upcoming show, run this promotion ladder:

- 2-3 weeks out: Announcement post (all 3 platforms)
- 1 week out: Reminder with urgency ("tickets going fast" or "almost sold out")
- Day before / day of: Final push with "tonight" or "tomorrow night" energy
- Post-show: Recap with crowd photos and patron reactions (all 3 platforms)
