# Artist Mappings

Maps each tribute act (as named in Airtable) to the original artist(s) they represent.
The script searches for news about BOTH the tribute act AND the original artist(s).
Leave the Original Artist cell blank if there's no applicable original artist.

## Search Precision Rules

- Always search the tribute act's **exact name** — do not broaden or paraphrase it.
- When searching original artist news, search for the **original artists themselves**,
  not tribute acts or cover bands for those artists.
- For acts with multiple original artists (e.g. Legends of Classic Rock), search each
  original artist individually as well as the tribute act name.
